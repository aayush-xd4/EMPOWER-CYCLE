PCOS Awareness

Home Remedies

1. Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

2. Regular exercise can help manage weight and improve insulin sensitivity.

3. Herbal teas like spearmint or green tea may help with symptoms.

4. Ensure adequate sleep and manage stress through relaxation techniques.

Normal Medicines

1. **Metformin**: Often prescribed to improve insulin sensitivity.

2. **Birth Control Pills**: Used to regulate menstrual cycles and reduce acne.

3. **Anti-Androgens**: Such as spironolactone, to reduce excessive hair growth.

Consult a Doctor

If you are experiencing severe symptoms such as extreme weight gain, severe acne, or significant fertility issues, please consult a healthcare professional for a personalized treatment plan.
